MOONFIT

Website Twitter Discord

MoonFit is a Web3 & NFT Lifestyle App that promotes active living by rewarding users anytime they burn calories through physical activities.


MoonFit users will start by purchasing MoonBeast NFT in the form of BEAST or BEAUTY, and do daily running activities through MoonFit App to earn tokens, which can be used for in-app upgrades, upgrade their NFTs, buy more NFTs for a better chance to earn more tokens, or cash out, HODL or trade for profit.


We're on a mission to promote an active lifestyle through Web3 & NFT. We encourage every form of training as long as calories are burnt. MoonFit pushes users to work out, keep fit, and stay healthy through our rewarding mechanism. We believe in positive values & positive changes that daily training will bring to the generation.


For that mission, applying Web3, NFT and Moonbeam ecosystem with a well-thought user rewarding mechanism is of utmost importance. We believe that technology of the future like NFT, Web3 and Moonbeam ecosystem can quickly help us reach our predetermined target. Besides, our real-life lifestyle/fitness application benefit and the proper rewarding system will inspire people to take one more step every day while helping us get one step closer to our goal.


MoonFit uses MFR token as the in-game token and MFG token as Governance Token.

SUBWALLET

Website

SubWallet is a user-friendly Web3 Multiverse Gateway for Polkadot and Kusama ecosystem. Our vision is to provide you with the simplest and most secure way to connect to blockchain-based applications like DeFi and GameFi on Polkadot.


SubWallet is built on top of Polkadot {.js} and focuses on improving user experience in the Polkadot and Kusama ecosystem—that is, we strive to make it easier for users to access and interact with Web3 applications that run on the Substrate Framework. In other words, we base SubWallet on Polkadot {.js}'s security backbone while improving all of its current drawbacks, including but not limited to:

  • Cumbersome infrastructure
  • Complicated user interface
  • High latency
This does not mean that we deny the contribution of Polkadot {.js}. It sets a crucial stepping stone for developers and plays a vital role for the Polkadot and Kusama ecosystem owing to its decentralization properties. Since there are too many blockchains and projects on the ecosystem that need to be managed, keeping track is immensely challenging for developers without a massive application like Polkadot {.js}. However, Polkadot end-users don't need this; they need something faster and more user-friendly. That's why SubWallet was born.
In the long run, we still believe that in the ecosystem, Polkadot {.js} is a pivotal platform application for developers. Our mission is to harness its power and bring it closer to the wider Polkadot community through user-friendly applications like SubWallet.


Our main features, or how we have improved from Polkadot {.js}:

  • Monitor multi-chain assets on extension window
  • Send and receive assets with a few simple taps
  • Grant access and verify transactions to Web3
  • Manage and transfer NFT assets
  • Manage portfolio
  • And many more soon
